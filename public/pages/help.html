Help & Guide
Getting started
  1. Create an account (Sign in → Sign up).
  2. Add a post: Sale / Exchange / Free.
  3. Browse and use filters (Category, Location, Size).
  4. Open an item and message the seller.
Safety tips
  • Meet in public places and check the item before paying.
  • Keep chat inside ReWear for a clear record.
  • Report suspicious behavior and block users if needed.
FAQ
Where do I see full item details?
Click “View” on any post to open the Item page with description, price/trade type, and actions.
How do favorites work?
Tap the heart icon (or “Add to favorites”). You can manage them from Favorites.
Why don’t I see new notifications?
Notifications update when you refresh. Unread count appears next to Notifications in the navbar.
